---
title: Illegal role name.
summary: Type roles should be one of representational, nominal, or phantom.
severity: error
introduced: 9.6.1
extension: RoleAnnotations
---

With the `RoleAnnotations` extension, [type role annotations](https://ghc.gitlab.haskell.org/ghc/doc/users_guide/exts/roles.html) can be added to type or class declarations. These must be one of `representational`, `nominal`, or `phantom`.
